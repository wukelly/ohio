Logistics

  • Registration opens at 8:00 AM on Sunday.
  • You must have your team registered by 9:30 AM to be judged.
  • Information needed to register:
    • Team Name
    • Team Member Names and E-mails
    • Project/Product Name
    • Project Description
    • Your Location (Room and Table ID)
    • Sponsor Challenge (if applicable)
  • Each team will be judged by 2 pairs of 2 judges, meaning 4 judges total. If your team has selected a sponsor challenge, you will be judged by another set of sponsor judges.
  • Prepare a short pitch/explanation of your project, each pair of judges will spend 10-15 minutes listening and asking questions.

Criteria

  • Technical Difficulty: How complex or difficult are the knowledge and skills needed to implement this project?
  • Functionality: What tasks does the project perform and how well does it execute these tasks?
  • Intuitiveness: Is the project easy for users to interact with? Is it easy for yourself or a new user to grasp?
  • Impact/Usefulness: Does the project have real-world impact? What communities will it affect in a meaningful way?
  • Craftsmanship: How is the quality of the build? Is it stable, clean, and aesthetically pleasing?
  • Creativity: Have you seen this idea somewhere else before? Is there something unique and different?
  • Wow Factor: Does the project captivate you or stimulate your fascination? Is there anything extra impressive about the amount of work, complexity, creativity, etc.?
  • Teamwork: How well did the students work as a team and share the required work equally?

Entrepreneurial Track

As MakeOHI/O grows, we also want to evolve! Although we love all projects made at Make, one of OHI/O's goal is to be able to provide resources for longer term, business oriented projects. If you think your project could become business, make a pitch! A requirement of choosing this challenge is to fill out a Lean Canvas worksheet about your business proposition.
